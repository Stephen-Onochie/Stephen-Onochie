FAQ

Common questions.

What services does Chevmic LLC offer?+

Painting (residential, commercial & industrial), electrical, outdoor projects, home repairs & handyman, office decoration, and all-in-one home maintenance — plus our sister division, Chevmic Events.

Do you offer same-day service?+

For most repairs and small jobs, yes — call before 11am and we'll usually have someone out the same day. Larger painting and outdoor projects are scheduled around your availability.

How do you price work?+

Project-based quotes for painting, outdoor and office work. Hourly ($85/hr, 1 hr minimum) for repairs and installations. Always quoted up front — no surprises.

Are you certified, licensed & insured?+

Yes — PDCA certified members, fully licensed and insured. Documentation provided with any quote.

Do you guarantee your work?+

Every job carries a one-year workmanship guarantee. If our fix fails within 12 months, we come back and re-do it at no charge.
