FAQ

Common questions.

Can I customize a package?

Always. The tiers are a starting point — most of our clients land somewhere between, and we'll build a proposal that matches exactly what you need.

Is a deposit required?

Yes — a deposit secures your date, with the balance due before the event. We'll walk through specifics on the consultation call.

Do you travel?

Within 90 miles of Avon, travel is included. Beyond that, a small travel fee applies — quoted transparently in your proposal.

Do you handle the rentals too?

Yes — we have an in-house rental inventory (chairs, tables, dinnerware) that integrates seamlessly with any package. See the catalog →

What if my event is bigger than 120 guests?

The Luxury tier accommodates events of any size. For 200+ guests, we'll build a custom proposal — reach out and we'll talk specifics.
